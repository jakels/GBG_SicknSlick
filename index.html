Why is Sick N' Slick the most trusted?

Sick N' Slick is a family run business founded in 1998 which provides quality vehicle body repair and paintwork services operating out of Armley in the Leeds area. Throughout our work and customer recommendations we have grown to become one of the best and most trusted motorvehicle repair and paintwork companies in the region.

All of our work is carried out by apprentice trained technicians with many years of experience with a range of vehicles and projects. So you know your vehicle is safe in our hands.
