Family run since 1998

Sick n Slick Motorcycle Cosmetics & Racepaint UK is a family run business and was founded in 1998 to provide a quality paintwork repair service in West Yorkshire. Through our work and recommendation we have grown to be one of the best motorcycle repaint companies in the region and are not only used by the general public, but are also the paint shop of choice for top local dealers such as Eddy Wright Harley Davidson & Triumph, Colin Appleyards and Powersport Bikes
